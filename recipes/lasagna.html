Lasagna

Description

A simple and delicious recipe for authentic lasagna. This recipe will keep your family coming back begging for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Lean ground beef
  • 8 oz Italian sausage with case removed
  • 2 Jars of pasta sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 kg Ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups Mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Parsley flakes
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 0.5 tsp Sea salt
  • 0.25 tsp Black pepper
  • 9 Lasagna noodles uncooked
  • .25 cups Grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F
  2. In a large skillet, brown ground beef and sausage, breaking up with a spoon.
    Drain the fat. Stir 4 cups of pasta sauce into the meat mixture; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat eggs. Stir in ricotta, 1.5 cups mozzerella, garlic powder, parsley,
    Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. To assemvle, spread about 1 cup of the meat sauce on the bottomm of a glass dish; line with 3
    of the noodles. Repeat once with meat sauce, noodles and cheese mixture.
    Top with remainiing noodles and meata sauce, making sure to cover noodles with sauce.
    Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella cheese and the parmesan cheese; cover with foil.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil; bake 15 minutes longer, or until noodles are tender and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Let stand for 15 minutes before serving.
