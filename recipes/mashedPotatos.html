Mashed Potatos

Description

Easy simple and buttery smooth, these mashed potatos are to die for!

Ingredients

  • 3 potatos
  • 1 tbls of butter

Instructions

  1. peel the potatoes
  2. place the potatos in a pot with water and bring to a boil
  3. let the potatos boil for 20 minutes
  4. Mash the potatos together with the butter, salt to taste
